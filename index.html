Chromebook Use Policy

Chromebooks are designed for educational use. They are to be used for schoolwork and research only. Students should not use them for personal use or entertainment.

Chromebooks must be treated with care. No food or drink should be near them while they are in use. If they are dropped or otherwise damaged, they should be taken to the school office for repair or replacement.

Students are responsible for any information they access on their Chromebooks. They should only log into websites and applications that are appropriate for school use. If they accidentally access something inappropriate, they should immediately close the window or tab and tell a teacher or parent.

Chromebooks should be turned off and stored in their cases when not in use. They should not be left in hot cars or other places where they could be damaged.

If a student needs to use a Chromebook for an extended period of time, they should ask a teacher or parent for permission. If they are allowed to use it for longer than an hour, they should take a break every 20 minutes to move around and stretch.
